What is Cold email?
Cold email is an initial email sent to a potential client or customer without prior contact or relationship. It's a powerful outreach tool used in business development, sales, and networking.
How to edit the personal details filled during registration?
You can edit your personal details by going to your Account Settings. Click on your profile picture, select "Settings", and navigate to the "Personal Information" tab where you can update your details.
What are the details necessary to generate a cold email?
To generate an effective cold email, you'll need: Name, Age, Course persuing or completed , Contact details, Email-id, Gender, Work experience(if any) and Skills you currently have.
For any issue, whom to contact?
For any technical issues or support, you can reach our dedicated support team at support@genmail.com or use the help center accessible from your dashboard. We typically respond within 24 hours.
